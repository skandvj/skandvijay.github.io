Optimizing eCommerce with Machine Learning: Predicting Customer Lifetime Value for Samsung

By Skand Vijay | Published on February 2025

Introduction

Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) is a critical metric for Samsung’s eCommerce business. Understanding CLV helps businesses determine the total revenue a customer is expected to generate throughout their relationship with the brand.

Why CLV Matters for Samsung?

  • Personalized Marketing: Understanding CLV helps Samsung tailor marketing campaigns for different customer segments.
  • Optimized Customer Retention: High-value customers can be targeted with special loyalty programs.
  • Strategic Resource Allocation: Samsung can allocate marketing budgets more efficiently by focusing on high-value customer segments.

How Machine Learning Enhances CLV Prediction?

Traditional CLV models rely on static calculations, but machine learning allows Samsung to analyze real-time data for better accuracy.

Machine Learning Models for CLV Prediction:

  • Regression Models: Predicting revenue based on past purchase behavior.
  • Neural Networks: Capturing complex relationships in consumer data.
  • Clustering Algorithms: Segmenting customers based on spending patterns.

Real-World Application for Samsung

By implementing ML-driven CLV prediction, Samsung can:

  • Increase Customer Retention: Personalized promotions for loyal customers.
  • Improve Sales Forecasting: AI-driven insights into purchase likelihood.
  • Optimize Subscription Models: AI-enhanced loyalty programs for Samsung’s digital services.
"AI-powered CLV prediction enables Samsung to maximize revenue by focusing on high-value customer engagement."

Final Thoughts

Samsung can leverage AI and machine learning to optimize customer relationships, increase retention, and drive revenue growth through intelligent CLV predictions.
