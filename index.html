About Me

Hello! I’m Skand Vijay, a driven professional and Master's student in Information Systems at Carnegie Mellon University. With a passion for integrating technology with business strategies, I am continuously learning to stay ahead of future trends. My academic journey includes advanced coursework like Generative AI, Intro to Deep Learning (a Ph.D.-level course), Data Science for Product Managers, and Product Management Essentials. To broaden my expertise, I’m also exploring Accounting and Financial Analytics, Managing Disruptive Technology, and leadership through my role as Vice President of the Analytics Club at CMU Heinz.

I led impactful projects, including automating financial processes to reduce a $10 million backlog risk and delivering business intelligence solutions that streamlined operations across teams.

When I’m not optimizing workflows or launching AI products, I’m deeply committed to my community. I take great pride in organizing events that foster learning and collaboration, and I actively mentor aspiring professionals, helping them achieve their academic and career goals. Engaging with the community energizes me, and I am passionate about supporting others on their journey toward success.
